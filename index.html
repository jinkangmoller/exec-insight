★ Key Insight

A recurring pattern: professionals who arrived skeptical left with a specific changed view. Vanessa Chua (engineer), Rachael Sim, and Brian Ng all arrived with "fluffy" or "buzzword" as a working assumption — and each documented a specific reversal.
